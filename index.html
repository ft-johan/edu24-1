Theme

Institute of Human Resources Development (IHRD) Kerala invites technical papers for presentation during its 2nd International Conclave on Generative AI and the Future of Education, which will be held in Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala. The conference aims to bring together researchers, academicians, scholars and industry professionals from around the globe to share their latest research findings, exchange ideas, and discuss emerging trends in AI and related applications. The conference will cover a wide range of topics related to applications of AI, including but not limited to

  • AI in Education and Learning
  • AI in Automation and Intelligent Systems
  • AI in Environmental Sustainability
  • AI in Finance, Banking & E-Commerce
  • AI in Healthcare & Biomedical Technologies
  • AI in Image/Signal/Text Processing
  • AI in Manufacturing and Industry
  • AI in Media & Entertainment
  • AI in Power Systems and Reliability
  • Ethical and Societal Implications of AI
